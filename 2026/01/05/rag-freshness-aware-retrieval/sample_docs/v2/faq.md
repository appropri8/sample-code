# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner and follow the prompts.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.

## Orders

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, you can cancel within 48 hours of placing the order.

**Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?**
A: Yes, gift wrapping is available for an additional $5.99.

## Returns

**Q: How do I return an item?**
A: Log into your account, go to Orders, and select the item to return.

**Q: What is the return window?**
A: You have 21 days from delivery to return items.

## Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

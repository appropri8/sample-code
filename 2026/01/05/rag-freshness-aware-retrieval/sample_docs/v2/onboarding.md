# Onboarding Guide

## Step 1: Create Account
Sign up with your email address or social media account.

## Step 2: Verify Email
Check your inbox and click the verification link.

## Step 3: Complete Profile
Add your name, preferences, and payment method.

## Step 4: Choose Plan
Select a pricing plan that fits your needs.

## Step 5: Take Tour
Complete the interactive product tour.

## Step 6: Start Using
You're ready to go! Check out our getting started guide.

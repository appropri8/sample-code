# Warranty Information

## Standard Warranty

All products come with a **2 year** limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

## What's Covered

- Manufacturing defects
- Material failures
- Component malfunctions
- Battery degradation (for applicable products)

## What's Not Covered

- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from misuse
- Unauthorized modifications

## Warranty Claims

Submit warranty claims through our support portal with proof of purchase. Claims are typically processed within 48 hours.

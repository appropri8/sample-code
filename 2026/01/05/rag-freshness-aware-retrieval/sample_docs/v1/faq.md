# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner and follow the prompts.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.

## Orders

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, you can cancel within 24 hours of placing the order.

## Returns

**Q: How do I return an item?**
A: Log into your account, go to Orders, and select the item to return.
